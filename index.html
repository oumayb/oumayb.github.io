Oumayma Bounou

New York University

New York, New York

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at NYU, where I am working with Yann LeCun on world models. Before that, I completed my PhD in the WILLOW project-team which is a joint team between Inria Paris, the Department of Computer Science of Ecole Normale Supérieure, and CNRS, where I was advised by Justin Carpentier and Jean Ponce.
Prior to that, I received an engineering degree from Télécom Paris.

My research interests lie in world models, control and optimization.
